    About

    Mirjana is a Serbian tango dancer. She discovered the capability of expressing herself, through dancing and music, at her early age. She danced Serbian traditional folklore dances, while her fortuitous encounter with tango in Belgrade in 2006, was a turning point of the definite continuation of her development as a dancer, in an Argentinian way.

    In collaboration with Miša Plavšić, the doyen of Serbia’s tango scene, with whom she began exploring this form of art, trained by Andrija Jovanović (DC Aster) and at seminars with top-notch instructors including Angel Coria, Demian Garcia, Aurora Lubiz, Jorge Firpo, Hugo Daniel, DNI Tango, Alejandra Mantinian, Gachi Fernandez, Natacha Poberaj, Gabriela Elias, Horacio Godoy in Buenos Aires, as well as with Ilias Oikonomou and Mauricio Castro in Hamburg, Mirjana has gained experience and enhanced her skills.

    She has more than 100 public appearances.
